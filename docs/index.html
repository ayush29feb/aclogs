Before the gym

Ask Claude what to lift

Claude knows your training history and your PRs. Ask it what weight to use today — it'll look at your last session for each lift and give you a target based on where you left off.

What should I squat today? Working up to a heavy set of 3.
Your last 3RM was 175 lbs on Mar 17. You've hit 185 for a single. Try opening at 155 → 165 → 175–180 for your top set. Push to 180 if it moves clean.
And bench?
Last bench session was 125×3 at RPE 8. Same weight or +5 — 130 lbs for 3 if you're feeling good.

At the gym

Log as you go

Write your workout in plain markdown during or after the session. Drop it in the workouts/ folder and run the import — Claude handles the rest.

I just finished. Back squat 135×5, 155×3, 175×3 @8. Deadlift 225×3, 245×3, 260×1 @9.
Got it. That's a new deadlift 1RM — 260 lbs. Adding it to your log now.

Importing workout · updating PRs…
✓ Session saved. 2 PRs updated: Deadlift 1RM 260, Back Squat 3RM 175.

Over time

Watch your strength compound

Every session feeds into your PR table. Filter by date range to see how you've progressed over the last month or year. Tap any lift to see your weight-over-time chart.
